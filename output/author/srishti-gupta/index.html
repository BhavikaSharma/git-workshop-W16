Research Showcase and Mixer

Wed 04 February 2015
   by Srishti Gupta

   in Events.

tags:  talks   academic   social 

Interested in academic computer science? Want to learn more about research? This session will showcase current graduate students' research fields and work.

Current UW graduate students will give a brief presentation of their research field, including security/privacy, formal methods, compilers, and programming languages. After the presentations, we'll have snacks available while you connect with them and get to know more in a mixer setting.

Event Details

  • Who: Cecylia Bocovich, Erinn Atwater, Carolyn Lamb, Laleh Ghoraie
  • What: Research Showcase and Mixer
  • Where: MC 5417
  • When: Wed. Feb 4, 5:30–7:00PM
