Clearpath Tech Talk

   in Events.

tags:  industry   talks 

Join us for a talk by female engineers from Clearpath Robotics! They will be discussing the challenges of building autonomous software for deployment in commercial/industrial spaces, describing their test infrastructure specific to robotics performance validation as well as good software practices they adhere to in order to produce robust and efficient software.

Everyone is welcome, and snacks will be provided!
